# Supply Chain Delay & Cost Leakage Assessment

*Translating 200K+ shipment records into a carrier reallocation recommendation*

![Dashboard Preview](images/dashboard-preview.png)

## Business Problem

An operations team knew delivery performance was degrading but had no way to isolate where or why, or which carriers were actually responsible.

## Approach

Analyzed over 200,000 shipment records to quantify delay patterns and cost leakage by carrier, turning "some carriers feel slower" into a measurable, defensible basis for a carrier reallocation recommendation.

## Tools

SQL, Power BI
