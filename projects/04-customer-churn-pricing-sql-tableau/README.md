# Customer Churn Risk & Pricing Gap Analysis

*28% of customers representing 44% of revenue were showing leading churn indicators*

## Business Problem

The business had sales data but no structured view of customer health. Standard reporting showed totals — this engagement needed to surface which segments were growing, which were at risk of churning, and whether pricing structure was leaving revenue uncaptured.

## Key Finding

A segment representing 28% of the customer base — but 44% of total revenue — was showing leading churn indicators: declining purchase frequency and widening gaps between orders.

## Approach

Built customer-level behavioral metrics (recency, frequency, pricing exposure) in SQL from raw transaction data, then visualized the segments in Tableau so stakeholders could interactively explore which cohorts were at risk.

## Tools

SQL, Tableau Public
