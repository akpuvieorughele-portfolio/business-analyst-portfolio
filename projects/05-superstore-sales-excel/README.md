# Superstore Sales & Regional Performance Analysis

*A self-service reporting tool revealing the gap between revenue rank and profit contribution*

![Dashboard Preview](images/dashboard-preview.png)

## Business Problem

Regional sales managers needed a self-service reporting view that went beyond raw transaction exports — one that surfaced segment performance, identified which regions were trading volume for margin, and allowed filtered views without requiring data team involvement.

## Approach

Built an interactive Excel workbook using PivotTables, PivotCharts, and slicers, designed to separate "how much revenue" from "how much profit" at a glance — a distinction raw transaction exports don't surface on their own.
