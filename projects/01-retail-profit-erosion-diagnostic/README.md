# Retail Profit Erosion Diagnostic

*How a single discount policy was silently destroying margin across 9,994 transactions*

![Dashboard Preview](images/dashboard-preview.png)

## Business Problem

A multi-region retail business had strong revenue but unexpectedly thin margins. The engagement began with a stakeholder question: "We know discounts are hurting us, but we don't know where or by how much." This project answers that question with evidence.

## Approach

Analyzed 9,994 transactions to isolate the relationship between discount level and realized profit, broken down by region, category, and discount tier — to find exactly where discounting stopped being a reasonable trade-off and started eating margin.

## Tools

Excel, SQL
